What we do

Creative-wall is dedicated to the manufacture of smart products. We eagerly apply the newest technologies in our design processes,throughout our super-efficient U.S. manufacturing facility. We design, engineer, and manufacture technically advanced products with an ancient, tried and true building material —durable, fire-safe rock.



3D Wall Panels

Three dimensional panels that work in multiples to create a continuous, uninterrupted sculptural wall. The proprietary, steel-reinforced joints interlock to ensure accurate panel-to-panel alignment and superior joint stability. Cast entirely of mineral, they're durable, safe and healthy.

3D Wall Tiles

Our new small-scale tiles are designed for tighter areas and more intimate spaces. Where our large-scale panels might be too unwieldy to install, or too large in scale, our new tiles offer the ideal solution!
